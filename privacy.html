Who we are

The Canada Citizenship Calculator (available at davevoh.github.io) is an independent, free informational tool that helps residents of Canada estimate their physical presence days toward Canadian citizenship eligibility. This site is not affiliated with the Government of Canada or Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).

Information we collect

This site is designed to be privacy-respecting. The information you enter into the calculator — your arrival date, PR date, and travel history — is processed entirely inside your own browser. We do not transmit these details to any server, store them on our systems, or share them with third parties. If you close the tab, the data is gone.

The only way your information leaves your browser is if you voluntarily submit the consultation request form. That form collects the name, email, phone number (optional), and the topic you selected, which are sent to a form delivery service so that a licensed Canadian immigration consultant can follow up with you.

Cookies and analytics

This site may use cookies for two purposes: (1) to display advertisements through third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense, and (2) to measure aggregate traffic. We do not use cookies to identify you personally.

Third-party advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on this site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits.

  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
  • For users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom, see youronlinechoices.com.

Third-party ad networks may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the time spent on each page. This information is used to serve ads that may be relevant to you.

Affiliate and sponsored links

Some links on this site are affiliate or referral links, which means we may earn a small commission (at no cost to you) if you click through and sign up for a service. These links are clearly labelled "Affiliate" or "Sponsored." We only recommend tools and services that we believe are genuinely useful to people navigating the Canadian immigration system.

Your rights

Because we do not store your calculator inputs on any server, there is no personal data for us to delete, export, or correct. If you have submitted the consultation form and would like your information removed, please email the address listed on the contact page.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of the page will reflect the most recent change. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent through the contact page.
